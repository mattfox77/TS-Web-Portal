## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the portal at your company's portal URL
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner
3. Choose your registration method:
   - **Email/Password**: Enter your email and create a secure password (minimum 8 characters with mixed case and numbers)
   - **Google**: Sign up using your Google account
   - **Microsoft**: Sign up using your Microsoft account
4. Complete your profile information
5. Verify your email address (check your inbox for verification link)

### Logging In

1. Visit the portal homepage
2. Click **Sign In** in the top right corner
3. Enter your credentials or use social login
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Click **Sign In**
2. Click **Forgot Password?**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Follow the link and create a new password

## Support Tickets

Support tickets are your primary way to request assistance and track issues.

### Creating a New Ticket

1. Click **Tickets** in the sidebar or **Create New Ticket** on the dashboard
2. Click **New Ticket** button
3. Fill in the ticket form:
   - **Title**: Brief description of the issue (5-200 characters)
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation of the problem (10-5000 characters)
   - **Priority**: Select urgency level
     - **Low**: General questions, minor issues
     - **Medium**: Standard support requests
     - **High**: Issues affecting productivity
     - **Urgent**: Critical system failures
   - **Project** (optional): Link ticket to a specific project
4. Click **Submit Ticket**
5. You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket ID

### Viewing Your Tickets

1. Navigate to **Tickets** in the sidebar
2. View all your tickets in a sortable table
3. Filter tickets by:
   - **Status**: Open, In Progress, Waiting on Client, Resolved, Closed
   - **Priority**: Low, Medium, High, Urgent
   - **Project**: Filter by specific project

### Ticket Details and Comments

1. Click on any ticket to view full details
2. View ticket information:
   - Current status and priority
   - Creation and last update dates
   - Assigned technician (if applicable)
   - Linked GitHub issue (for technical projects)
3. **Add Comments**:
   - Scroll to the comment section
   - Type your message in the text box
   - Click **Add Comment**
   - You'll receive email notifications when staff respond

### Ticket Status Meanings

- **Open**: Ticket submitted, awaiting review
- **In Progress**: Technician is actively working on your issue
- **Waiting on Client**: We need additional information from you
- **Resolved**: Issue has been fixed, awaiting your confirmation
- **Closed**: Ticket is complete and closed

### Best Practices for Tickets

✅ **Do**:
- Provide detailed descriptions with steps to reproduce issues
- Include error messages or screenshots when relevant
- Respond promptly when status is "Waiting on Client"
- Set appropriate priority levels

❌ **Don't**:
- Create duplicate tickets for the same issue
- Mark everything as "Urgent" (this delays truly urgent issues)
- Include sensitive passwords in ticket descriptions

## Documents

The document management system provides secure storage and sharing of project files.

### Uploading Documents

1. Navigate to **Documents** in the sidebar
2. Click **Upload Document** or drag files into the upload area
3. Select file(s) from your computer
4. Choose options:
   - **Project**: Associate document with a specific project (optional)
   - **Description**: Add notes about the document (optional)
5. Click **Upload**

**File Requirements**:
- Maximum file size: 50MB per file
- Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, TXT, ZIP, PNG, JPG, and more
- Files are encrypted and stored securely

### Viewing and Downloading Documents

1. Navigate to **Documents**
2. Browse your document library showing:
   - Filename
   - File size and type
   - Upload date
   - Associated project
   - Uploaded by (user name)
3. Click **Download** to retrieve a file
   - Download links are valid for 1 hour for security
   - Links automatically regenerate when needed

### Organizing Documents

- Use the **Filter by Project** dropdown to view project-specific files
- Sort by filename, date, or size
- Search for documents by name

### Deleting Documents

1. Find the document you want to remove
2. Click the **Delete** button (trash icon)
3. Confirm deletion
4. **Note**: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

## Invoices and Payments

Manage your billing and make secure online payments through the portal.

### Viewing Invoices

1. Click **Invoices** in the sidebar
2. View all invoices in a table showing:
   - Invoice number (format: INV-YYYY-NNNN)
   - Issue date and due date
   - Total amount
   - Payment status (Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue, Cancelled)
3. Filter invoices by:
   - Status
   - Date range

### Invoice Details

1. Click on any invoice to view full details
2. Review invoice information:
   - Company and billing information
   - Line items with descriptions, quantities, and prices
   - Subtotal, tax, and total amounts
   - Payment status and transaction details (if paid)
   - Notes from Tech Support

### Downloading Invoice PDFs

1. Open the invoice you want to download
2. Click **Download PDF** button
3. Save the PDF to your computer for your records

### Making Payments

**Online Payment via PayPal**:

1. Open an unpaid invoice
2. Click **Pay with PayPal** button
3. You'll be redirected to PayPal's secure checkout
4. Choose your payment method:
   - PayPal balance
   - Credit/debit card
   - Bank account
5. Review payment details
6. Click **Complete Payment**
7. You'll be redirected back to the portal
8. Invoice status will update to "Paid"
9. You'll receive a payment receipt via email

**Payment Confirmation**:
- Immediate invoice status update
- Email receipt with transaction ID
- Payment recorded in your payment history

### Payment History

1. Navigate to **Payments** in the sidebar
2. View all payment transactions:
   - Payment date
   - Invoice number
   - Amount paid
   - Payment method
   - Transaction ID
3. Download receipts for individual payments
4. View year-to-date payment total

### Understanding Invoice Status

- **Draft**: Invoice is being prepared (not yet sent)
- **Sent**: Invoice has been issued and is awaiting payment
- **Paid**: Payment has been received
- **Overdue**: Payment due date has passed
- **Cancelled**: Invoice has been voided

## Subscriptions

Manage your recurring service subscriptions and automatic billing.

### Viewing Active Subscriptions

1. Click **Subscriptions** in the sidebar
2. View all active subscriptions showing:
   - Service package name
   - Billing cycle (Monthly or Annual)
   - Next billing date
   - Subscription amount
   - Status (Active, Cancelled, Expired, Suspended)

### Subscribing to a Service Package

1. Visit the **Services** page from the main navigation
2. Browse available service packages
3. Review package features and pricing
4. Click **Subscribe** on your chosen package
5. Select billing cycle:
   - **Monthly**: Billed every month
   - **Annual**: Billed once per year (usually discounted)
6. Click **Continue to PayPal**
7. Authorize the subscription in PayPal
8. Return to the portal to see your active subscription

### Managing Subscriptions

**Viewing Subscription Details**:
- Current status
- Start date
- Next billing date
- Payment history for this subscription

**Cancelling a Subscription**:

1. Navigate to **Subscriptions**
2. Find the subscription you want to cancel
3. Click **Cancel Subscription**
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period
6. You'll receive a confirmation email

**Important Notes**:
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period
- No refunds for partial periods
- You can resubscribe at any time
- Automatic invoices are generated for each recurring payment

### Subscription Billing

- Automatic payments are processed on the billing date
- You'll receive an invoice via email after each payment
- Failed payments will trigger email notifications
- After 3 failed payment attempts, subscriptions may be suspended

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Can't Log In

**Problem**: "Invalid credentials" error

**Solutions**:
- Verify you're using the correct email address
- Check Caps Lock is off
- Try password reset if you've forgotten it
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

#### Not Receiving Emails

**Problem**: Missing ticket confirmations or invoice emails

**Solutions**:
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Add noreply@techsupportcs.com to your contacts
- Verify your email address in Settings
- Check notification preferences are enabled
- Contact support if issue persists

#### File Upload Fails

**Problem**: Document upload doesn't complete

**Solutions**:
- Check file size is under 50MB
- Verify file format is supported
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Disable browser extensions temporarily

#### PayPal Payment Issues

**Problem**: Payment doesn't complete or invoice doesn't update

**Solutions**:
- Ensure you completed the PayPal checkout process
- Check your PayPal account for the transaction
- Wait 5-10 minutes for webhook processing
- Check your email for payment confirmation
- Contact support with your transaction ID if issue persists

#### Can't See My Projects/Tickets

**Problem**: Expected items don't appear in lists

**Solutions**:
- Check filter settings (status, date range)
- Verify you're logged into the correct account
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if data is genuinely missing

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Access

**Q: Can multiple people from my company access the portal?**
A: Yes! Contact Tech Support to add additional users to your client account. Each user will have their own login credentials.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, update it in Settings. You'll need to verify the new email address.

**Q: How long do sessions last?**
A: Sessions remain active for 7 days of inactivity. You'll be automatically logged out after this period for security.

### Tickets & Support

**Q: How quickly will my ticket be answered?**
A: Response times vary by priority:
- Urgent: Within 2 hours during business hours
- High: Within 4 hours
- Medium: Within 24 hours
- Low: Within 48 hours

**Q: Can I attach files to tickets?**
A: Not directly in the ticket form, but you can upload documents and reference them in your ticket description.

**Q: What happens when a ticket is marked "Resolved"?**
A: Review the resolution and confirm it works. If the issue persists, add a comment and we'll reopen it.

### Billing & Payments

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept PayPal, credit cards (via PayPal), debit cards, and bank transfers through PayPal.

**Q: Do I need a PayPal account to pay?**
A: No, you can pay with a credit/debit card as a guest through PayPal's checkout.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refund policies vary by service. Contact support to discuss your specific situation.

**Q: Are there late fees for overdue invoices?**
A: Late fee policies are outlined in your service agreement. Contact billing for details.

### Subscriptions

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription?**
A: Yes, contact support to change your service package. Changes typically take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens if my payment fails?**
A: You'll receive an email notification. Update your payment method in PayPal. After 3 failed attempts, your subscription may be suspended.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Subscription pausing depends on your service agreement. Contact support to discuss options.
